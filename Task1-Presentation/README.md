
Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Overview
As a Student Digital Ambassador, my first assignment was to create a one-page visual resource to help my peers understand the importance of digital literacy. This infographic serves as an introductory guide for first-year B.Tech students to navigate the digital world safely and professionally.


Infographic Details

Tool Used: Canva.


Target Audience: First-year B.Tech students across all branches.


Key Topics Covered:

Definition of Digital Literacy.

Essential digital tools for students.

Safe internet practices and cyber hygiene.

Contents of this Folder

infographic-screenshot.png: A high-resolution export of the final design.


design-description.txt: A brief summary of the design choices and the tool's features.


Reflection Summary
The creation of this infographic allowed me to practice "visual storytelling"—distilling complex topics like email etiquette and professional online presence into a single, scannable page. Using browser-based tools like Canva demonstrated that professional design is accessible even without prior graphic design experience.
