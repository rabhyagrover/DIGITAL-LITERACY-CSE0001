Task 1: Design Description
Tool Used: Canva

Design Title: Your Guide to Digital Literacy: Cyber Security

Visual Overview: The infographic utilizes a dark navy-blue background with a subtle world map overlay, symbolizing the global nature of the digital landscape. I chose a high-contrast color palette—white and orange text against a dark background—to ensure maximum readability on mobile devices and desktop screens.

Key Design Elements: * Visual Hierarchy: The title "CYBER SECURITY" is set in a bold, sans-serif typeface to immediately grab the viewer's attention. Smaller, colored headers like "Key Skills" and "Staying Safe Online" guide the eye to supporting information.

Central Graphic: I incorporated a modern vector illustration showing a desktop and mobile device protected by a shield. This reinforces the theme of cross-platform safety and digital protection.

Structured Content: Information is broken down into three distinct quadrants:

Key Skills: Highlighting critical thinking and responsible use.

Staying Safe Online: Providing actionable tips like using strong passwords.

Importance: Listing the long-term benefits such as career success and lifelong learning.

Interactivity & Engagement: The bottom of the poster features a clear, grounding mission statement: "Understanding, Using, and Creating a Digitally Responsible World." This acts as a call to action for the viewer to move beyond being a passive user to an active, responsible digital citizen.
