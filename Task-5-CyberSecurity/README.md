This module focuses on understanding the mechanics of cybercrime and establishing proactive defense habits.
ContentsCase Study: Phishing – A detailed breakdown of a realistic "Urgent KYC Update" scam.
Stay Safe Online Checklist – Actionable tips for securing digital and financial identities.
Key Learning: The Phishing MechanicsThis task involved researching how attackers use social engineering to bypass technical security.
Targeting: Students and digital payment users.
Method: Impersonating trusted entities (banks/UPI providers) via SMS or email.
Goal: Theft of credentials, OTPs, and financial assets.
Emergency Reporting ChannelsIf you or someone you know is a victim of cybercrime in India, use these official channels immediately:
* National Cyber Crime Helpline: Dial 1930 (24/7).
* Official Portal: cybercrime.gov.in.
Reflection Summary:
The most surprising element of this research was discovering that cyber-safety is more about human psychology than complex coding.
By slowing down and verifying links before clicking, we can stop the majority of common attacks.
