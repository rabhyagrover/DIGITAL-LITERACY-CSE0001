The "Do’s" of Social Media✅:
* Do Curate a Professional Profile: Keep your LinkedIn and professional bios updated with your current year, major, and key projects.A clean, high-quality profile picture goes a long way.
* Do Network Meaningfully: Use platforms to follow industry leaders, join academic groups, and engage with content related to your field.
* Do Highlight Your Achievements: Share your certifications, event photos, or successful projects. 
* Do Use Privacy Settings Wisely: Regularly audit your privacy settings on personal accounts.
* Do Fact-Check Before Sharing: In an era of rapid information, take ten seconds to verify a story or a "viral" tip before reposting it.
  




The "Don'ts" of Social Media❌ :
* Don’t Post in the "Heat of the Moment": Avoid posting when you are angry or frustrated, especially regarding campus politics or personal disagreements. 
* Don’t Share Sensitive Personal Info: Never post your student ID number, specific dorm room location, or private contact details.
* Don’t Complain About Employers or Faculty: Even if an internship is stressful or a class is difficult, vent to friends offline. 


* Don’t Engage with "Trolls": Avoid getting into long, aggressive comment wars.
* Don’t Forget the "Grandmother Rule": Before hitting post, ask yourself: "Would I be comfortable with my grandmother or a future hiring manager seeing this?" If the answer is no, keep it in your drafts.
